Installation the 4FP

These steps will guide you to configue your device.

Step 1

Insert the groove all the way and fold it to insert the screw.

Step 2

Allen screw M8 X 16.

Step 3

Allen screw M8 X 30.

Step 4

For connecting wide profiles, carriage bolts M10 X 35.

Tighten the nuts as much as possible, the pressure plate should be concave inward.

Step 5

For a narrow center profile, use M8 X 30 carriage bolts.

Step 6

In the left and right grooves of long profiles you must place load-bearing vertical profiles and on the M8 X 30 carriage bolts.

Step 7

For long profiles, a vertical auxiliary profile should be placed in the central groove and onto the M8 x 30 carriage bolts.

Step 8

Note that the panels are connected in series. It is important to observe the correct order of their placement, as in the picture - otherwise the cables will not reach.

Step 9

Use the M8 X 16 Allen screw to install the controller.

Connect the output wires from the electrical panels to the terminals exiting the left and right of the electrical box.

Step 10

Bolt with head.

Step 11

Step 12

Carriage bolts M8 X 50/60/70 with washers.

You need to tighten it to the maximum, the hook can bend at the same time - that's right.

Step 13

Allen screw M8 X 25 with washers on the nut side. You need to tighten as tightly as possible, the fact that the clamps bend is the right thing.

Step 14

Then connect to enter the home Wi-Fi network name and access password.

Step 15

Stellen Sie dann eine Verbindung her, um den Namen des Wi-Fi Heimnetzwerks und das Zugangspasswort einzugeben.

Step 16

Next, the device automatically connects to receive a table of angle values for your location and time from the server.

Step 17

If your ISP doesn't provide your approximate location, select your location manually in the app settings menu.

Step 18

If you need to limit the opening angle of the panel, go to manual control in the settings and set the maximum opening angle of the device.
